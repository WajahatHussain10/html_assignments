Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB), officially Royal Challengers Bengaluru

Before the 2024 Indian Premier League, the team was renamed Royal Challengers Bengaluru

Royal Challengers Bangalore also hold two important IPL records, for the lowest score in an innings in IPL (49, against the Kolkata Knight Riders) and for the highest score conceded in an innings (287, against Sunrisers Hyderabad).
